Annual leave or vacation time : A set amount of paid time off that employees can use for personal or vacation purposes. Sick leave : Paid time off for employees who are ill or have a medical condition. Personal leave: Paid time off for personal reasons, such as a family emergency or bereavement. Parental leave: Paid time off for new parents to care for a newborn or newly adopted child. Maternity leave: Paid time off for expectant mothers to give birth and recover. Paternity leave: Paid time off for expectant fathers to bond with a newborn or newly adopted child. Family and Medical Leave Act (FMLA) leave: Unpaid time off for employees who need to care for a family member or themselves due to a serious health condition. Bereavement leave: Paid time off for employees who have suffered the loss of a family member. Jury duty leave: Paid time off for employees who are serving on a jury. Military leave: Paid time off for employees who are serving in the military or who are called up for active duty. Holidays: Paid time off for recognized holidays, such as Christmas or Thanksgiving.
